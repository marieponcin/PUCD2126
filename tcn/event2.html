The club’s main room hosts not one, not two, but three fantastic DJ-producers—any one of them would make for a gotta-be-there night, but together they make up a lineup that’s close to mind-blowing. The Berlin-based Detroiter Jimmy Edgar, cofounder of the Ultramajic label, has created a singular discography of electro-funk rhythms over his career, ranging in sound from near-alien iciness to R&B-tinged carnality. Mike Dunn, meanwhile, has been a hugely respected mainstay of the Chicago house scene almost since there’s been a Chicago house scene, and he has the selecting skills to prove it; Brooklyn’s own Jovonn is right up there with Dunn in house-music veneration, with such stone-cold deep-beat classics as “I Can’t Make Up My Mind” and “Don't Wanna Let U Go” to his name.
